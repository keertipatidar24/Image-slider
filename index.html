Seven Wonder
This list was compiled through a global poll conducted by the New7Wonders Foundation in 2007:
Chichen Itza
A large pre-Columbian archaeological site built by the Maya civilization, famous for the Pyramid of Kukulkan, an astronomical and architectural marvel.
Machu Picchu
A 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Andes Mountains. Renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone construction and stunning mountain views.
Christ The Redeemer
A towering statue of Jesus Christ overlooking Rio de Janeiro. Symbolizes peace and stands 98 feet tall, with arms stretched wide.
Petra
An ancient city famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system. Known as the "Rose City" due to its pink sandstone cliffs.

The Great Wall of China
A massive wall built to protect against invasions and raids, stretching over 13,000 miles. It showcases ancient China's military ingenuity.
Taj Mahal
A white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Renowned for its stunning architectural beauty and intricate details.
